Granny's Date Balls

Date Balls

Meep Meep

Meep Meep christmas

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup chopped dates
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups rice crispies

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cream butter, egg, and sugars together and then add dates. Cook in a pot over low heat for 10 minutes and stir.
  2. Cool then add vanilla and rice crispies
  3. Roll into small balls (slightly smaller than a golf ball) the roll in icing to sugar coat.
  4. Refrigerate or can be frozen.
